Welcome to Fished it, Mate!

A community-driven blog dedicated to all fishing enthusiasts! Whether you're a seasoned angler or just starting out, this platform is designed for like-minded individuals who share a passion for fishing...

How It Works

At Fished it, Mate!, we believe in making information accessible and engaging. Here's how you can navigate our blog:

Create a profile: The first thing to do is register and create a profile. This allows you to create posts and keep track of your fishing information...
Type of Fish Caught: Add your favourite fish in your profile, and the ability to add a fish type when you make a blog to discuss your fishing session...
Fishing Method Used: Whether you prefer fly fishing, deep-sea fishing, or float waggler...

Join us in celebrating the art of fishing! Share your stories, learn from others, and contribute to a vibrant community...
